About the System

The SMS Phishing Detection System was developed to address the growing issue of phishing scams targeting mobile users. These sophisticated scams often attempt to deceive individuals into sharing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card details, and personal data through seemingly legitimate SMS messages.

With our advanced AI-powered system, users can easily analyze suspicious SMS messages in real-time to detect potential threats and ensure their digital safety. Our platform combines machine learning algorithms with comprehensive threat intelligence to provide accurate and reliable detection.

A Brief History

Phishing scams date back to the early days of the internet but have evolved significantly with the rise of mobile communication. Today, scammers target victims via SMS, often using sophisticated social engineering tactics to create urgency and fear, making it increasingly difficult to distinguish legitimate messages from fraudulent ones.

This system is designed to provide a quick and reliable way to analyze and detect these fraudulent attempts, leveraging advanced algorithms including Naive Bayes classifiers and LSTM neural networks to identify patterns and threats with high accuracy.
